Matcha Latte

Description

Matcha latte is a creamy, vibrant green drink made from Japanese matcha tea powder. Itâ€™s earthy, smooth, and naturally energizing. perfect for mornings or study sessions.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon matcha powder
  • 2 ounces hot water (not boiling)
  • 8 ounces milk (dairy or plant-based)
  • 1-2 teaspoons sweetener (honey, sugar, or syrup), optional

Instructions

  1. Whisk matcha powder with a small amount of hot water until smooth.
  2. Heat the milk until warm and frothy.
  3. Pour the milk into the matcha mixture.
  4. Add sweetener to taste.
  5. Serve hot or iced.
