Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a hearty Italian pasta dish made with layers of rich meat sauce, creamy cheese, and tender pasta sheets. It’s comforting, flavorful, and perfect for family dinners. This dish is baked until the top is melted and golden, creating a warm, satisfying meal everyone loves.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat until fully cooked. Drain any excess fat.
  4. Add the minced garlic to the beef and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  5. Stir in the marinara sauce, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Let it simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. In a bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, and half of the Parmesan cheese. Mix well.
  7. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom.
  8. Layer 4 lasagna noodles over the sauce.
